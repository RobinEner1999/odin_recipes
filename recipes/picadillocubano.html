Picadillo Cubano

Descripcion

Picadillo Cubano is a flavorful dish made with ground beef, onions, peppers, and tomatoes, seasoned with cumin and other spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes and cumin.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve hot with rice or as a filling for tacos or empanadas.
